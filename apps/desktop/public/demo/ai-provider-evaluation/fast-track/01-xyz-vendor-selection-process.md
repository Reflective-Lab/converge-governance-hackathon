# XYZ Vendor Selection Process

## Job To Be Done

When the buyer is selecting AI providers for agentic software work, evaluate the available vendors and operating patterns so the organization can adopt a governed provider setup that is auditable, cost-aware, and resilient.

## Desired Outcome

Select a provider strategy for the next two quarters that supports:

- Discussion and synthesis for product, engineering, and governance teams.
- Agentic coding and evaluation workflows.
- Stakeholder-ready summaries for procurement and leadership.
- Escalation to deeper reasoning only when risk or ambiguity warrants it.
- Evidence capture for every promoted decision.

## Candidate Provider Patterns

1. Single premium model for all tasks.
2. Small/efficient model for most tasks plus premium escalation.
3. Multi-provider mix through a router such as Kong or OpenRouter.
4. Direct provider integrations without a gateway.

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Evidence Needed |
|---|---:|---|
| Task fit | 30 | Model role benchmark, synthesis quality, coding-agent reliability |
| Governance and audit | 25 | Logging, policy enforcement, data controls, gateway support |
| Cost predictability | 20 | Token forecast, routing strategy, escalation frequency |
| Resilience | 15 | Provider fallback, rate-limit behavior, regional availability |
| Adoption speed | 10 | Existing keys, integration effort, operational familiarity |

## Decision Boundary

The recommendation must not choose a provider setup that lacks a clear audit trail for prompts, model choices, and promoted facts.

## Human Gate

Procurement review is required before promoting the final provider strategy unless a Cedar delegation exists for the same risk class, budget envelope, and evidence coverage.
